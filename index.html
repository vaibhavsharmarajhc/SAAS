Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

At CounselAI (Chambers), we understand that legal practitioners handle highly sensitive and confidential data. Protecting your chamber's privacy and your clients' cases is our highest priority.

1. Data Storage & Hosting

All your chamber data, case records, financial ledgers, and client lists are securely hosted on MongoDB Atlas cloud servers. The database is isolated per tenant, ensuring that your data remains completely confidential and inaccessible to other chambers.

2. Encryption Guardrails

All data processed by Chambers is encrypted in transit using secure HTTPS protocols (SSL/TLS). Confidential case materials, client details, and passwords are also encrypted at rest within our databases to prevent unauthorized disclosures.

3. Data Export & Ownership

You retain 100% ownership of your data. You can download a complete backup of your case lists, financial logs, and client sheets in a standard JSON format at any time directly from the System Settings panel.

Our Mission

Scaling Law Practice with Intelligence

"Most lawyers lack data about the growth of their own practice and thus struggle to scale it to top-tier law firms. Chambers bridges this analytics gap to help you build a data-driven chamber."

In the legal profession, managing caseloads manually creates an operational ceiling. Advocates spend hours parsing physical listing boards, tracking outstanding billing ledgers, and manually copying case briefs for client updates. This manual administrative overhead locks lawyers in a loop of administrative friction, preventing them from growing their practice.

How Chambers Powers Practice Scaling

01

Data-Driven Client Growth & Referrals

By recording referral sources for every case, Chambers tracks exactly which channels bring in your highest-yielding litigation work. This visibility allows advocates to target business relationships and networking efforts where they yield the maximum return.

02

Plugging Revenue Leakage with Ledgers

Chambers categorizes payment statuses (Billed, Received, Outstanding, Disbursed, WrittenOff). The dashboard flags high outstanding accounts instantly, enabling prompt billing collection reminders. This structured approach helps advocates reduce outstanding dues by up to 35% in the first quarter.

03

Operational Leverage through Delegation

Instead of storing case updates in a physical diary, Chambers puts everything in a secure cloud workspace. Advocates can easily delegate diary entries, listings, and invoicing work to junior associates, allowing the firm to scale and handle larger case volumes effortlessly.
